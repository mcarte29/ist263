Banana Bread Recipe

  • 3 ripened bananas
  • ½ cup butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt

Directions:

Brown the butter on medium heat. Mash the ripened bananas in a bowl. Mix in the brown sugar and the browned butter. Mix in the egg and vanilla and once incorporated add in all the dry ingredients.Preheat the oven for 350 degrees and bake for 1 hour.
